# Review Cadence And Quality Metrics

## 1) Review Cadence

- Weekly: incident and defect trend check.
- Monthly: dependency drift and quality debt review.
- Quarterly: architecture and quality posture review.

## 2) Core Metrics

- Defect escape rate and customer-facing incident count.
- SLO attainment and error-budget consumption.
- Release rollback frequency and mean time to recover.
- Accessibility and performance trend for key journeys.

## 3) Improvement Loop

- Convert recurring issues into structural improvements.
- Re-baseline targets after major architecture or product changes.
