# Output And Handoff

## Recommended Output Structure

- Problem statement and constraints.
- Evidence summary with file references and source links.
- Option comparison with tradeoff table.
- Recommendation with rationale.
- Rollout, rollback, and test gate plan.
- Open questions and follow-up tasks.

## Quality Gates Before Handoff

- Ensure every material claim has evidence.
- Ensure risks are concrete and actionable.
- Ensure unresolved uncertainty is explicit.
- Ensure recommendation aligns with constraints and timelines.
